Minimal Techno from the Underground

Based in Finland. Available for club & festival bookings worldwide.

KELLARI is a hungry, hardworking, and endlessly curious Finnish-American- Canadian DJ and music producer, driven by a deep love for underground house and techno. With a strong foundation in mixing, mastering, and event marketing, KELLARI approaches music not just as sound but as a space for freedom, presence, and introspection.

Bridging the worlds of technology, psychology, and club culture, KELLARI creates tracks and sets that move bodies and minds alike. Each project is a study in emotional energy, human connection, and the raw pulse of the dancefloor.
