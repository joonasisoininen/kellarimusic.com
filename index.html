KELLARI is a Finnish-American-Canadian DJ and producer blending multiple house and techno styles with emotional depth and sonic precision.

With a background in computer science, psychology, and business, he approaches music as both a craft and a space for connection, movement, and introspection. His sets and productions are driven by raw grooves, stripped-back textures, and a love for the dancefloor’s unifying energy.

As a co-founder and resident DJ of Pulsify Collective, KELLARI helps lead a growing grassroots movement focused on inclusivity, creative expression, and building awareness of house music culture in Canada. Pulsify Collective organizies pop-up parties and nightclub events that blend community, sound, and space in unexpected ways. Pushing boundaries with format, location, and vibe. From curation to performance, Pulsify Collective contributes to shaping an experience rooted in connection, authenticity, and underground energy.

Recent original releases like “Same Vibration” with Ron Carroll and Soleil Carrillo and “I Feel Like” have earned support from names like Don Diablo, Jamie Jones, Shiba San, and David Penn show that his sound resonates across borders and genres.
